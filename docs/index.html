CNN & video clipping

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

One of the most popular deep learning neural network, has already outperformed humans at object recognition.

In addition to self-driving cars, CNN is also used at Cubesole AI to recognize millions of objects in the video clips and recommend more accurate ads.

NLP & AI assistant

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Research

New

1. EagleEye Architecture

Our first in-house AI architecture that is specialized at identifying millions of objects from video clips.

EagleEye Architecture

Built on Computer Vision, EagleEye aims to deliver the most relevant ads to users.

State-of-the-art Computer Vision

Computer vision is used by self-driving cars, public safety, IoT, and many other fields. We believe computer vision will also take heavy part at producing relevant ads on social network.

Convolutional Neural Network

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN), which is the deep learning architecture behind computer vision, allows us to identify millions of objects, and their brands, shapes, colors, which we then use to inform the ads recommendation system, providing more targeted and real-time ads.

Pensieve Architecture

Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)

New

3. Jasmine

Jasmine is the Alexa-like AI assistant that will place reservations, orders, and bookings automagically for the groups.

Jasmine

Jasmine, the Alexa-like AI assistant, aims to be your Jarvis (from Iron Man) in real life.

AI assistant

One of the most well known AI application is its ability to play the role of human-like assistant. Jasmine provides its users the ability to book reservations, set up meetings, host events, and so much more.

Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing, which is also used at Google for Google Translate and its Reservation API, is the deep learning neural network that powers Jasmine. Jasmine uses NLP to understand user's reply and order, and fine tune the services along the way.

4. Trumpbot

Trumpbot is a side project for studying and emulating Donald Trump's behaviours with AI.

Trumpbot

Trumpbot is a side humour project that uses AI to emulate Donald Trump behaviours.

Tweets

The number of tweets produced by Donald Trump has presented a fertile ground for training bots that emulate Donald Trump provided in a certain context.

Wider Applications

Bots training based on rich number of text has wider applications, including not just Donald Trump but the users of Cubesole. We believe rich chat history will provide us a glimpse on how to make Jasmine even more powerful at guessing users' next move.
