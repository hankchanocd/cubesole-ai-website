Research

New

1. EagleEye Architecture

EagleEye specializes at identifying millions of objects from images and video clips, and produce intelligent tagging.

EagleEye Architecture

Built on Computer Vision, EagleEye aims to deliver the most relevant image search results to users.

Computer Vision & Tagging

Computer vision is used by self-driving cars, public safety, IoT, and many other fields. We are using computer vision to place intelligent tagging on images and video clips. The tags lead users to intelligent image search results.

Convolutional Neural Network

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN), which is the deep learning architecture behind computer vision, allows us to identify millions of objects, their brands, shapes, colors, and more. We then use them to provide targeted image search results, which is often a list of best-matched merchants and service providers.

New

2. Pensieve Architecture

Equipped with GANs, Pensieve creates AI-generated contents that unleash the creativity of our users.

Pensieve Architecture

Pensieve, which is still under development, is perhaps the most creative aspect of Cubesole infrastructure, providing AI-generated recommendation based on the content

Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)

Generative Adversarial Network creates impressive AI-generate artwork, often mixing up two pieces of artworks.


Although the applicability of GANs is unclear as of now, we believe GANs have potentials to disrupt the industry in the time to come. The recent players at GANs include the popular FaceApp and the scandalous DeepFake.

New

3. Jasmine

Jasmine, our flagship event planning intelligence, places reservations, orders, and bookings automagically for groups.

Jasmine

Jasmine, the Alexa-like AI assistant, aims to be your event planning intelligence.

Event Planning Intelligence

One of the most well known AI application is to play human-like assistant. Jasmine takes a step further to provide the ability to book reservations, set up meetings, host events, and so much more from service providers.

Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing, which is also used at Google for Google Translate and Google Reservation API, is the deep learning neural network that powers Jasmine. Jasmine uses NLP to understand users' reply and order, and automate what would have been weeks or months of planning.

4. Trumpbot

Trumpbot is a side project for studying and emulating Donald Trump's behaviours with AI.

Trumpbot

Trumpbot is a side humour project that uses AI to emulate Donald Trump behaviours.

Tweets

The number of tweets produced by Donald Trump has presented a fertile ground for training bots that emulate Donald Trump provided in a certain context.

Wider Applications

Bots training based on rich number of text has wider applications, including not just Donald Trump but the users of Cubesole. We believe rich chat history will provide us a glimpse on how to make Jasmine even more powerful at guessing users' next move.
